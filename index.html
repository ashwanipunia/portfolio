About Me

As a resourceful and dedicated biochemist, I have over five years of experience in research using mass spectrometry. I specialize in metabolomics, proteomics, and lipidomics using mass spectrometry with chemometric analysis. My expertise includes a strong background in a variety of chromatographic and spectrometric instruments, such as HPLC, UPLC, MALDI-TOF-TOF-MS, and GC-MS. I am dedicated to developing novel extraction, isolation, and separation techniques for biomolecules.
